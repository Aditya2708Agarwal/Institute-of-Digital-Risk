About IDR

An Industry-Led Institute for a High-Stakes Discipline

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

We operate at the intersection of academic insight and real-world practice. Through a formal partnership with a leading UK university, IDR embeds research-backed methodology into every programme, ensuring practitioners graduate with both theoretical foundations and the practical tools demanded by employers.

Unlike traditional training providers, IDR is not a classroom. It is a career pipeline — taking talent from initial training through certification, live project exposure and direct industry placement.

Mission
Build a sustainable pipeline of qualified digital risk professionals for regulated industries.
Academic Partnership
Formal collaboration with a UK university, embedding research rigour into practical programmes.
Industry Practice
All programmes informed by practitioner experience in NIST, ISO 27001, NIS2 and AI governance.
Deployment
Graduates placed into advisory, in-house and project roles within partner organisations.

Who We Serve

A Community Built Around Practitioners

IDR exists to serve individuals at every stage of their digital risk career — from students exploring the field to experienced professionals seeking advanced credentials in a rapidly evolving discipline.

Our programmes are designed specifically for those operating in, or aspiring to join, regulated and high-consequence environments where the cost of inadequate risk management is measured not just in financial terms, but in systemic impact.
